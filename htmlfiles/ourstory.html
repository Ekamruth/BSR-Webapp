OUR STORY

BSR Power Constructions pvt ltd is founded in the year 1982 in the town Tenali of Guntur district in Andhra Pradesh state. It started its role as service provider for electrical needs and later extended to manufacturing of electrical power transformers and poles. Over the years, our group has gained a great position in the market and later extended our services to government of Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Jharkhand, Assam, West Bengal and North-eastern states with our subsidiary units. Expertise and professionalism as our tools we are committed for establishing a sustainable position in the industry by meeting the requirements of our clients and governments.

As an electrical solution specialist, we provide electrical power products, Provides transformers, switchgears, breakers. Our team is composed of Qualified, technically skilled, and highly dedicated personnel working cohesively to promote value-added solutions with a competitive pricing structure to suit industry needs. We also engage in electrical power station commissioning, preventive maintenance, automation, and energy saving solutions. Our commitment is to deliver outstanding results that will exceed market expectations.
