BSRPCPL owes its success primarily to the unstinted support given by its satisfied customers. It believes in serving its customers for life time – be it offering quality, price, performance and convenience. Its track record in keeping its customers for life time has made it the undisputed market leader in both the telugu states . It enjoys the reputation of being a credible and capable vendor to all its customers. Its customers include very large corporate buyers to very small retail buyers.

BSRPCPL’s customer segments are broadly classified into: Government, Corporate and Retail. The Government segment includes government-controlled State Electricity Boards and Power Distribution Companies. The Corporate segment covers all major companies that take up large projects in the power transmission and distribution sector. The retail segment includes all those contractors who buy poles in small quantities for the purpose erecting new poles or replacing old pole.
